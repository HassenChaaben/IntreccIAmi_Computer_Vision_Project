**intrecciami-style, Intreccio semplice, Intreccio semplice con trama doppia, Natural Rattan, 2 mm Strand Width, 20 mm Post Spacing, Yellow and Purple Colors, Handcrafted Woven Texture**

A vibrant handcrafted woven panel featuring a simple intreccio technique with alternating yellow and purple natural rattan strands. The pattern showcases a double weave structure with evenly spaced purple posts and a repeating over-under crossing of yellow and purple wefts, creating a striking geometric design within a wooden frame.
