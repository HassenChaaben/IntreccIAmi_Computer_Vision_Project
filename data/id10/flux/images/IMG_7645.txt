intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials. The frame is crafted from light-colored wood, providing a simple yet elegant border that highlights the intricate weaving within. The weave itself is composed of alternating strands of pink and white, with the pink posts made of natural, round rattan filaments measuring 2 mm in diameter, spaced 18 mm apart. These posts are interlaced with wefts that include both pink rattan filaments and white mignon leather strands, each approximately 2 mm thick. The wefts create a visually appealing wave-like pattern, with each strand carefully woven over and under the posts in a consistent, repeating sequence. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, preserving the natural texture and color of the materials. The tactile quality suggests a soft yet sturdy weave, with a balanced density that allows for both visual interest and structural integrity. The alignment of the strands is precise, ensuring a uniform and aesthetically pleasing result.
