intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials. The weave is composed of alternating posts and wefts, creating a visually striking pattern. The posts are made of natural leather (strobel), measuring 2 mm in thickness, spaced 15 mm apart, and arranged in a straight, vertical alignment. These posts serve as the structural foundation, providing stability to the weave. The wefts, comprising a combination of natural leather fettuccia (6 mm wide) and strobel (2 mm wide), are intricately interlaced over and under the posts in a diagonal crisscross pattern. This arrangement results in a dense, uniform texture with a consistent repeat pattern, enhancing both the visual appeal and tactile quality. The weave maintains a natural, unvarnished (grezzo) finish, highlighting the raw beauty of the materials. The overall structure is framed by a simple, light-colored wooden border, which accentuates the intricate weave within. The piece exudes a rustic yet refined aesthetic, embodying the essence of traditional craftsmanship.
