intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its intricate and symmetrical pattern. The weave is composed of natural leather strands, specifically mignon leather in a rich, earthy "Terre bruciate" color, which translates to "burnt earth." The posts and wefts are both 8 mm in size, creating a uniform and sturdy structure. The strands are woven in a tight, over-under pattern, resulting in a dense and consistent texture with a grid-like appearance. The alignment of the strands is precise, ensuring a balanced and aesthetically pleasing design. The tactile properties suggest a firm yet flexible material, with the natural grain of the leather adding a subtle textural detail. The overall finish is unadorned, allowing the natural beauty of the leather to shine through, making it a testament to traditional craftsmanship.
