intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio technique, featuring a harmonious blend of simplicity and elegance. The weave is constructed using two distinct sets of strands: vibrant yellow and deep violet. The posts, made of natural, round rattan filaments, are a consistent 2 mm in thickness and are spaced evenly at 20 mm intervals, creating a structured and orderly foundation. The wefts, also 2 mm in diameter, alternate between yellow and violet, interlacing over and under the posts in a simple yet intricate pattern. This alternating color scheme results in a visually striking checkerboard effect, with each crossing point meticulously aligned to ensure uniformity and symmetry. The natural finish highlights the raw beauty of the materials, emphasizing the tactile texture and the artisanal craftsmanship. The overall density is moderate, allowing the weave to maintain both flexibility and structural integrity, making it a perfect example of traditional Intreccio semplice and Intreccio semplice con trama doppia techniques.
