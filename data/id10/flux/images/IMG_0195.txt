intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a beautiful example of the Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its intricate and deliberate weave construction. The structure is built using natural rattan strands, each with a round profile and a diameter of approximately 2 mm. The posts are evenly spaced at 17 mm intervals, providing a consistent and sturdy foundation. The wefts, also made of natural rattan, are woven over and under the posts in a simple yet elegant pattern, creating a tight and uniform weave with a balanced density.

The overall form is inspired by the shape of the Gigì fan, giving it a unique, organic silhouette. A striking feature of this piece is the concentric embroidery at the center, crafted with black rafia thread. This machine-made embroidery adds a bold contrast to the natural rattan, forming a series of nested, circular patterns that draw the eye inward. The weave maintains a clean and precise alignment throughout, with the strands appearing smooth and evenly spaced, enhancing the tactile quality of the piece. The finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, preserving the natural texture and color of the rattan, which adds to its artisanal and rustic charm.
