intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials and vibrant colors. The structure is composed of round rattan posts in a fuchsia hue, evenly spaced at 20 mm intervals, providing a sturdy foundation. The wefts alternate between fuchsia round rattan strands and wider, flat rattan pieces in a soft pink shade, creating a rhythmic visual pattern. Interspersed among these are flat, white leather strips, adding a contrasting texture and color. The weave is tight and uniform, with each strand carefully interlaced over and under the posts, resulting in a dense, structured fabric. The overall finish is natural and unvarnished, highlighting the raw beauty of the materials and the intricate craftsmanship involved. The piece is neatly framed within a light wooden border, enhancing its artisanal appeal.
