intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio spina salto 2 technique, characterized by a tight and uniform interlacing pattern. The weave is composed of natural raffia strands, specifically the bark of the palm (Corteccia di Giunco), each strand measuring 4 mm in size. The posts and wefts are intricately woven in an over-under pattern, creating a consistent and dense texture with a repeating diamond-like structure. The strands are evenly spaced, ensuring a balanced and symmetrical appearance throughout the piece. The finish is a dark green paint (Verde scuro, RAL 6012), which enhances the natural texture of the raffia while providing a sleek, uniform color. The overall tactile quality suggests a slightly rough yet sturdy surface, typical of handwoven natural materials. The craftsmanship is evident in the precise alignment and uniformity of the weave, making it both functional and aesthetically pleasing.
