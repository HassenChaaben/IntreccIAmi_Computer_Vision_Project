intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The weave is composed of natural rattan piattina strands, meticulously interlaced to create a uniform and visually appealing texture. The posts, measuring 5.75 mm in size, are spaced 13 mm apart, providing a consistent and structured foundation. The wefts, slightly thicker at 6 mm, are woven through the posts in a tight, repeating pattern, ensuring a dense and durable construction. The entire piece is finished with a smooth, marrone (brown) paint, giving it a rich, earthy tone that enhances its natural aesthetic. The overall alignment is precise, with each strand carefully placed to maintain the integrity of the weave, resulting in a tactile surface that is both sturdy and inviting. The craftsmanship highlights the beauty of traditional weaving techniques, making it a timeless and functional piece.
