intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven panel showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The structure features vertical posts made of natural rattan, each with a round profile and a diameter of 4 mm, spaced evenly at 15 mm intervals. These posts serve as the primary framework, providing stability and a consistent grid-like foundation. The wefts, also crafted from natural rattan with a round profile, are slightly thinner at 2.5 mm, woven tightly and uniformly across the posts to create a dense, uniform texture. The weave is tight and precise, with each strand crossing over and under the posts in a consistent, repeating pattern, resulting in a sturdy and tactile surface. The natural finish of the rattan gives the piece a raw, organic appearance, emphasizing the material's inherent texture and color. Adding a unique artistic touch, the panel features a sublimatic print of a destriero (a medieval warhorse) designed by Antonio Aricò, which is delicately overlaid on the woven surface, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern artistic expression. The overall composition is both functional and decorative, highlighting the beauty of handwoven textures and the integration of artistic elements.
