intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant interlacing pattern. The weave is constructed using natural, leather-like fettuccia strands, each measuring approximately 4 mm in width. The posts are neatly aligned at a consistent distance of 15 mm apart, providing a structured foundation for the weaving. The wefts, also in black, are woven over and under the posts in a straightforward over-under pattern, creating a tight and uniform texture. The overall density of the weave is moderate, with the strands interlocking seamlessly to form a cohesive and durable fabric. The edges of the piece are slightly frayed, adding a rustic, artisanal touch, while the central woven section displays a clean, grid-like pattern that highlights the precision of the Intreccio semplice technique. The tactile properties suggest a firm yet flexible material, ideal for both decorative and functional applications.
