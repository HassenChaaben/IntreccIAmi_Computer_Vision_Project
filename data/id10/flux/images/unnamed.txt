intrecciami-style: This intricate woven piece showcases a harmonious blend of natural leather strands in varying widths and colors, meticulously interlaced to create a visually striking pattern. The weave is executed using the intreccio technique, where posts and wefts are carefully arranged to form a complex, layered structure. The posts are composed of wide, 6 mm leather fettuccia strands in a pristine white color, spaced 20 mm apart, providing a sturdy foundation. The wefts include a mix of materials: white leather fettuccia strands of the same 6 mm width, thinner white mignon strands at 3 mm, and delicate brown mignon strands at 2 mm, all woven in an alternating pattern to add depth and texture. The strands are woven in an over-under sequence, creating a dense yet flexible fabric with a balanced spacing that allows the natural grain of the leather to be visible. The weave features a repeating pattern that radiates outward, with the strands crossing each other in a dynamic, star-like formation. Adding a unique touch, silver metal rings with an external diameter of 26 mm and a thickness of 3 mm are integrated into the weave, serving as both structural elements and decorative accents. The overall finish is natural, highlighting the raw beauty of the leather and the precision of the weaving technique, resulting in a tactile and visually captivating piece.
