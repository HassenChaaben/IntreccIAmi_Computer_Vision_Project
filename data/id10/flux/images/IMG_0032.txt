intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials. The weave is composed of alternating rows of round rattan strands, with posts and wefts both measuring 2 mm in diameter. The posts, dyed in a soft, warm shade of pink, are evenly spaced at 19 mm intervals, creating a consistent and structured foundation. Interspersed between these pink strands are white wefts made from natural strobel, which add a striking contrast and visual rhythm to the pattern. The over-under crossings are meticulously executed, resulting in a tight, uniform weave with a balanced density. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, highlighting the natural textures and colors of the materials. The tactile quality suggests a smooth yet slightly organic feel, emphasizing the artisanal craftsmanship and the natural beauty of the rattan and strobel.
