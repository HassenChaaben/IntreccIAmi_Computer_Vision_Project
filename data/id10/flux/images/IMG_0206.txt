intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural rattan strands. The posts, made of round, natural rattan filaments with a diameter of 1.75 mm, are evenly spaced at 2 mm intervals, providing a sturdy vertical framework. The wefts, also crafted from natural rattan filaments with a slightly larger diameter of 1.88 mm, are woven over and under the posts in a simple, alternating pattern, creating a dense and uniform texture. The weave is tight and consistent, highlighting the natural color and texture of the rattan material. Adorning the surface is a digital print featuring a repeating diamond pattern in vibrant shades of blue, light blue, red, and light green, adding a modern, decorative touch to the traditional woven structure. The overall finish is natural, with no additional paint or coating, emphasizing the authentic and tactile qualities of the rattan. The alignment of the strands is precise, contributing to the piece's balanced and aesthetically pleasing appearance.
