intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant over-under pattern. The structure features a combination of natural rattan strands and blue leather wefts, creating a striking visual contrast. The rattan posts and wefts, both round and measuring 2.5 mm in diameter, are woven in a tight, uniform pattern, ensuring a dense and sturdy construction. Interspersed among the natural rattan are thicker blue leather wefts, approximately 3 mm in diameter, which add a bold accent and a touch of sophistication. The weave alternates between the natural rattan and blue leather in a repeating pattern, creating a visually appealing and tactilely engaging texture. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, highlighting the natural textures and colors of the materials. The alignment is precise, with each strand crossing cleanly over and under its neighbors, resulting in a balanced and harmonious design. The piece exudes a rustic yet refined aesthetic, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern design elements.
