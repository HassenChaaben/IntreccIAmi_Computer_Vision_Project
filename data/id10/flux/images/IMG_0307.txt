intrecciami-style: This intricate woven piece showcases a harmonious blend of natural and metallic elements, crafted using the Intreccio technique with a Vario weave pattern. The structure features thin, natural strands of bamboo or similar material, each approximately 2 mm in size, woven in a complex over-under pattern that creates a grid-like foundation. The posts and wefts are evenly spaced, maintaining a consistent density throughout the piece, which contributes to its sturdy yet flexible nature. A standout feature is the integration of golden metallic rings, each with a diameter of 18 mm, strategically placed at the intersections of the weave. These rings not only serve as decorative elements but also enhance the structural integrity of the piece. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, preserving the natural texture and color of the materials. The tactile quality suggests a combination of the smoothness of the metal rings and the organic roughness of the bamboo strands, creating a visually and physically engaging texture. The alignment of the weave is precise, highlighting the craftsmanship and attention to detail in this handcrafted item.
