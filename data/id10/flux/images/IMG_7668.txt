intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The weave is composed of natural rattan piattina strands, each measuring approximately 6 mm in size, creating a uniform and sturdy texture. The posts are evenly spaced at 13 mm intervals, providing a consistent and structured foundation for the wefts to interlace. The wefts, also made of the same natural rattan material, are woven through the posts in a tight, alternating pattern, ensuring a dense and cohesive structure. The entire piece is finished with a glossy brown lacquer, enhancing its durability and giving it a rich, polished appearance. The overall design is symmetrical and repetitive, with a clean alignment that highlights the craftsmanship and attention to detail. The tactile properties suggest a firm yet slightly flexible texture, ideal for both functional and decorative purposes.
