intrecciami-style: This image showcases a meticulously crafted woven texture featuring the traditional Intreccio semplice technique. The weave is composed of natural, spaccato (split) rattan strands, each approximately 3 mm in diameter, creating a harmonious blend of earthy tones. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, providing a structured foundation for the wefts to interlace. The wefts, also made of the same natural rattan material, are woven in a simple over-under pattern, creating a consistent and repetitive design that emphasizes the natural texture and grain of the rattan. The entire surface is finished with a gray paint (Verniciatura), giving it a modern, industrial aesthetic while maintaining the organic feel of the natural material. The overall density is moderate, allowing for a balanced visual and tactile experience, with the strands aligned neatly to ensure uniformity and durability. The finish enhances the texture, adding a subtle sheen that highlights the intricate weave and the natural beauty of the rattan.
