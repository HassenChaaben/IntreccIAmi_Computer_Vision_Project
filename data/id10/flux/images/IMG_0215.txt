intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural and dyed materials. The structure is composed of vertical posts made from natural raffia (Corteccia di Giunco), each measuring 2.5 mm in size and spaced 12 mm apart, creating a sturdy and evenly distributed framework. The wefts, alternating between light blue (Azzurro) and dark blue (Blu scuro) leather strips, are woven in a simple over-under pattern, creating a visually striking and rhythmic design. The leather wefts are also 2.5 mm in size, ensuring a consistent texture and density throughout the piece. The natural raffia posts and leather wefts are interlaced with precision, resulting in a balanced and symmetrical weave. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, highlighting the raw, organic texture of the materials. The tactile properties suggest a combination of softness from the leather and the natural, slightly rough texture of the raffia, making it both visually appealing and engaging to the touch. The alternating color pattern of the wefts adds a dynamic visual element, enhancing the piece's aesthetic appeal while maintaining the structural integrity of the weave.
