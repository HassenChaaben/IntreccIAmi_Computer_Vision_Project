intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural rattan strands. The posts are composed of round, natural rattan fibers, each measuring 2 mm in diameter, spaced consistently at 19.75 mm intervals. These vertical posts serve as the structural foundation, providing a sturdy base for the weaving. The wefts, also made of natural rattan and the same diameter, are woven horizontally in an over-under pattern, creating a tight and uniform weave with a consistent density. The interplay of the posts and wefts results in a grid-like structure, emphasizing the simplicity and elegance of the Intreccio semplice method.

Adding a touch of modernity and visual interest, a digital print has been applied over the woven surface. The print features a checkerboard pattern in soft shades of pink and light green, which contrasts subtly with the natural tones of the rattan. The pattern is evenly distributed, enhancing the piece's aesthetic appeal without compromising the integrity of the weave. The overall finish is natural, with no additional paint or coating, allowing the inherent texture and color of the rattan to shine through. The tactile properties suggest a smooth yet slightly textured surface, inviting touch and interaction. This piece beautifully merges traditional weaving techniques with contemporary design elements, resulting in a versatile and visually striking artisanal creation.
