intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a delicate and intricate design, featuring a natural straw base with a scortechiata (peeled) finish, giving it a raw, rustic texture. The weave is characterized by a tight, uniform pattern of interlaced strands, creating a grid-like structure with consistent spacing and alignment. Overlaid on this base is a decorative rinfilo technique, where two distinct weft materials are used to create a wavy motif. The first weft, a thin, light yellow natural or leather strand (3 mm in size), is interwoven with a thicker, black fettuccia (6 mm in size), forming a rhythmic, serpentine pattern that adds visual interest and depth. The contrasting colors and varying thicknesses of the wefts create a dynamic interplay, while the overall construction remains sturdy and well-crafted, emphasizing the artisanal quality of the piece. The non-painted, natural finish enhances the organic and tactile appeal of the material.
