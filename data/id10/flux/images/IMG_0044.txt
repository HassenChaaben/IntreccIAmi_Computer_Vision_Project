The natural color of the rattan strands is preserved, emphasizing the raw and organic aesthetic. The weave exhibits a tight and even density, with each crossing point clearly defined, resulting in a visually appealing and tactilely engaging surface. The overall finish is unvarnished, highlighting the natural beauty and texture of the rattan material. This piece exemplifies the classic elegance and craftsmanship of the Intreccio semplice technique.
