intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural and leather-like materials. The posts, made from mignon di pelle scamosciata fucsia, are uniformly sized at 4 mm and spaced 15 mm apart, creating a structured and symmetrical framework. The wefts, also crafted from the same fucsia-colored mignon material, interlace with the posts in a simple over-under pattern, resulting in a dense and cohesive weave. The strands are evenly distributed, with no visible gaps, ensuring a smooth and tactile finish. The overall construction highlights the natural texture and softness of the material, while the vibrant fucsia color adds a bold and eye-catching element to the piece. The edges are neatly finished, maintaining the integrity of the weave and emphasizing the artisanal quality of the work.
