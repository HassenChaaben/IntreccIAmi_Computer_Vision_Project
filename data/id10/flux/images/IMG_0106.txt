intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique, characterized by a Vario weave pattern. The structure is composed of natural leather fettuccia strands, dyed in a vibrant Rosso (red) hue, creating a rich, uniform color throughout. The posts and wefts are both made from the same material, each strand measuring approximately 10 mm in size, ensuring a consistent and sturdy weave. The strands are interlaced in a traverso (transverse) pattern, where the wefts cross over and under the posts in a regular, repeating sequence, forming a grid-like texture with square-shaped intersections. The weave is dense, with minimal spacing between the strands, resulting in a tightly woven surface that appears both durable and tactile. The natural texture of the leather is visible, adding depth and character to the piece, while the uniformity of the weave suggests skilled craftsmanship and attention to detail. The overall finish is natural, highlighting the inherent qualities of the leather material.
