intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven panel showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The weave is constructed using natural, split rattan strands, both for the posts and wefts, which are uniformly sized at approximately 3.43 mm and 3.5 mm, respectively. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, creating a consistent and structured grid-like foundation. The wefts are woven through these posts in a regular, alternating over-under sequence, resulting in a tight and uniform weave with a balanced density. The overall finish is a subtle, water-based paint in the shade VERDE RD 160.80.15, giving the piece a soft, light green hue that enhances its natural texture and craftsmanship. The alignment of the strands is precise, contributing to the panel's clean and artisanal aesthetic. The tactile properties suggest a smooth yet slightly textured surface, ideal for both decorative and functional applications.
