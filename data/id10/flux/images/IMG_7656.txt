intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural rattan strands. The weave is composed of posts and wefts, both made from spaccato (split) rattan, each strand measuring 3.5 mm in diameter. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, providing a structured foundation for the wefts to interlace. The wefts cross over and under the posts in a simple, alternating pattern, creating a consistent and uniform texture. The overall weave density is moderate, allowing for a balanced visual and tactile experience. The piece is finished with a glossy, verniciatura (lacquered) paint in a soft, rose-colored hue, enhancing its elegant and refined appearance. The finish gives the woven surface a smooth, lustrous sheen, highlighting the natural beauty of the rattan while adding a touch of modern sophistication. The alignment of the strands is precise, ensuring a clean and professional look, making this piece both visually appealing and tactilely engaging.
