intrecciami-style: This woven piece showcases a harmonious blend of natural materials and vibrant colors, crafted using the Intreccio technique with a focus on Intreccio semplice and Giro a 2 weave types. The structure features vertical posts made of natural rattan filo tondo, each 2 mm in size, evenly spaced at 17 mm intervals, providing a sturdy foundation. The wefts are composed of alternating materials: flat piattina strands in yellow and pink, measuring 5 mm in size, and round filo tondo strands in natural, yellow, and pink, all 2 mm in size. The wefts are woven in a repeating pattern, with the yellow and pink piattina strands creating a wavy, undulating effect across the surface, while the natural filo tondo strands provide a contrasting straight weave. The special description highlights the giro a 2 pattern, where the yellow and pink filo tondo strands are intricately interwoven in a double-turn technique, adding depth and complexity to the design. The overall finish is natural, with no paint applied, emphasizing the raw beauty of the materials. The weave is dense yet flexible, with a balanced spacing that allows for both structural integrity and visual appeal. The tactile properties suggest a smooth yet slightly textured surface, inviting touch and interaction. This piece exemplifies a meticulous and artistic approach to traditional weaving techniques, resulting in a visually striking and tactilely engaging creation.
