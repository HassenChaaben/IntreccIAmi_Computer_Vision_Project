intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The weave is composed of natural rattan piattina strands, meticulously interlaced to create a uniform and visually appealing texture. The posts, measuring 8 mm in size, are evenly spaced at 12 mm intervals, providing a consistent structural foundation. The wefts, slightly thinner at 7.85 mm, weave through the posts in a tight, regular pattern, ensuring a dense and sturdy construction. The entire piece is finished with a smooth, matte brown paint, giving it a rich, earthy tone that enhances its natural aesthetic. The overall alignment is precise, with each strand carefully placed to maintain the integrity of the weave, resulting in a tactilely satisfying and visually harmonious design.
