intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous application of the Intreccio technique, specifically employing the Giro a 2 and Giro a 2 scontrato weave types. The structure features natural rattan posts, each 2 mm in diameter, evenly spaced at 15 mm intervals, creating a consistent and orderly framework. The wefts, also made of natural rattan and 2 mm in thickness, are woven in a tight, alternating over-under pattern that creates a visually appealing and tactilely engaging texture. The wefts are woven in a way that they cross over and under the posts, forming a dense and uniform weave with a slight wave-like appearance due to the Giro a 2 scontrato technique. Adding a decorative and functional element, each post is adorned with a round, silver-colored bead, which enhances the artisanal aesthetic and provides a subtle contrast against the natural rattan. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, preserving the raw, natural look of the materials. The piece exudes a rustic yet refined charm, blending traditional weaving techniques with subtle embellishments.
