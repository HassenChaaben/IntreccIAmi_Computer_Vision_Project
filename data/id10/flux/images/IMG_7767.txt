intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a traditional intreccio technique, featuring a natural, earthy palette with tones of brown and green. The weave is constructed using 5 posts and 5 wefts, creating a sturdy and symmetrical pattern. The posts are made from natural material, approximately 4 mm in diameter, while the wefts are slightly thinner at about 3.73 mm. The strands are tightly interlaced in an over-under pattern, resulting in a dense and uniform texture. The weave exhibits a consistent repeat pattern, with each post and weft crossing cleanly and precisely, ensuring structural integrity and visual harmony. The finish is grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, highlighting the natural texture and raw beauty of the materials. The tactile properties suggest a slightly rough yet durable surface, ideal for applications such as chair upholstery or decorative elements. The overall craftsmanship demonstrates a blend of functionality and aesthetic appeal, characteristic of traditional weaving techniques.
