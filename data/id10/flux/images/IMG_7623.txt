intrecciami-style: This woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique with a Jacquard weave pattern, creating a visually striking and geometrically precise design. The weave is composed of natural leather strands, featuring two distinct colors: a lighter tan (Marrone chiaro) for the posts and a deeper brown (Marrone) for the wefts. Both strands measure 4 mm in size, contributing to a consistent and uniform texture throughout the piece. The posts and wefts are intricately interlaced in an over-under pattern, forming a repeating geometric motif that adds depth and complexity to the surface. The strands are tightly woven, with minimal spacing between them, resulting in a dense and sturdy fabric. The natural finish of the leather strands enhances the tactile quality, offering a smooth yet slightly textured feel. The overall alignment is precise, highlighting the craftsmanship and attention to detail in this handcrafted woven texture.
