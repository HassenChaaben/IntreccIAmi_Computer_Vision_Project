intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a simple yet elegant over-under pattern. The structure is composed of natural, split rattan strands, each approximately 3.5 mm in diameter, creating a balanced and uniform texture. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, providing a consistent framework for the wefts to interlace. The wefts, also made of natural split rattan, are woven in a straightforward crisscross pattern, ensuring a tight and durable weave. The overall density is moderate, allowing for both structural integrity and a breathable appearance. The piece is finished with a water-based paint in a soft, diluted camelia color, giving it a light, almost off-white hue that enhances its natural aesthetic. The edges are neatly framed by a painted wooden border, adding a clean and finished look to the artisanal design. The tactile properties suggest a smooth yet slightly textured surface, ideal for both decorative and functional use.
