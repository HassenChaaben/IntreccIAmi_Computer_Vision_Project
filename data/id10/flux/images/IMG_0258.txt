intrecciami-style: This exquisite piece showcases a sophisticated intreccio weave technique, specifically employing the intreccio semplice and giro a 2 methods. The structure is crafted using synthetic cordino strass, featuring a uniform size of 5 mm for both posts and wefts. The material is uncoated (grezzo), maintaining a natural, non-painted finish that highlights the inherent shimmer of the strass. The weave pattern is characterized by a dense, repetitive arrangement of the cordino strass, creating a textured, sparkling surface. The posts and wefts are meticulously interlaced, with the giro a 2 technique adding a final layer of complexity and elegance. The overall design includes a five-pass corcia, enhancing the structural integrity and visual appeal. The cordino strass emits a subtle cry effect, adding a delicate, reflective quality to the piece. The color palette is dominated by a soft, gray hue, contributing to a refined and luxurious aesthetic. The tactile properties suggest a combination of rigidity from the synthetic base and a delicate, sparkling texture from the strass, making it a visually striking and tactilely engaging item.
