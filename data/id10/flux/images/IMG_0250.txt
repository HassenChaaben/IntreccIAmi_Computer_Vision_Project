intrecciami-style: This handcrafted macramé piece showcases a delicate and intricate weave, crafted using natural cotton cord with a twisted texture. The cord, measuring 2 mm in diameter, is meticulously interlaced to form a grid-like pattern with a consistent, open weave structure. Each intersection is secured with a floral knot technique, creating a harmonious and symmetrical design. The weave features evenly spaced crossings, resulting in a uniform and airy texture that allows light to pass through, enhancing its visual appeal. The edges of the piece are adorned with long, soft tassels, adding a bohemian touch and a tactile contrast to the structured weave. The natural color of the cotton cord gives the piece a warm, earthy tone, making it both visually soothing and texturally inviting. The overall construction is a testament to the skillful art of macramé, blending functionality with aesthetic beauty.
