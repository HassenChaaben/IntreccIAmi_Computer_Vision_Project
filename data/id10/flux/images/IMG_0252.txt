intrecciami-style: This intricately woven piece showcases a harmonious blend of natural leather strands in varying thicknesses, meticulously interlaced to create a visually striking pattern. The weave features a combination of "Pattern a X" and "Vario" techniques, resulting in a dynamic and textured surface. The posts are composed of 4 mm leather strands in a light blue (Azzurro) hue, spaced 13 mm apart, forming a grid-like structure. These posts are crossed over by wefts of the same material and color, creating a consistent "X" pattern throughout the weave. Interspersed within this grid are thinner, 2 mm black (Nero) leather strands that add depth and contrast, weaving through the blue structure in a more organic, flowing manner. The weave is further accentuated by the inclusion of golden rings with an external diameter of 18 mm, strategically placed at the intersections of the blue strands, adding a luxurious and decorative element. The overall construction is dense yet flexible, with a balanced spacing that ensures both structural integrity and aesthetic appeal. The tactile properties suggest a smooth yet slightly textured surface, with the interplay of colors and materials creating a sophisticated and artisanal finish.
