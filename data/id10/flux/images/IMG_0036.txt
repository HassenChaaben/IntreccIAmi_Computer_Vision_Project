intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice con trama tripla technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials and a striking color contrast. The weave is composed of round rattan posts in a vibrant azure hue, spaced evenly at 18 mm intervals, providing a sturdy vertical framework. The wefts alternate between flat and round rattan strands, with the flat strands in a rich brown color and the round strands in matching azure, creating a visually dynamic and tactilely engaging pattern. The brown flat wefts are woven over and under the azure posts in a consistent, repeating sequence, while the azure round wefts are interlaced between the flat wefts, adding depth and complexity to the weave. The overall structure is dense yet flexible, with the strands maintaining a uniform thickness and alignment, resulting in a cohesive and balanced texture. The natural, unvarnished finish enhances the organic feel of the rattan, highlighting the raw beauty and craftsmanship of the piece.
