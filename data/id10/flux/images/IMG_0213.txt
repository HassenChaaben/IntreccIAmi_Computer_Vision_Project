intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice con trama doppia technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural and dyed materials. The structure is composed of vertical posts made from natural rattan filo tondo, dyed in a deep Blu scuro, with a diameter of 1.85 mm. These posts are evenly spaced and serve as the primary framework. The wefts consist of two distinct layers: the first layer is made of natural cortecchia di giunco strands, 4 mm in size, providing a sturdy base with a warm, earthy tone. The second layer is formed by thinner, 2 mm rattan filo tondo strands, also dyed in Blu scuro, which weave over and under the natural wefts, creating a visually striking double weave pattern. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, highlighting the natural textures and colors of the materials. The weave is tight and uniform, with consistent over-under crossings that result in a durable and aesthetically pleasing texture. The tactile properties suggest a combination of smoothness from the dyed rattan and a slightly rougher, organic feel from the natural cortecchia di giunco, making it both visually appealing and tactilely engaging.
