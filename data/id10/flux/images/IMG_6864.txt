intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio technique, specifically the Intreccio spina salto 2 pattern. The weave is composed of natural, flattened rattan strands, each measuring approximately 8 mm in thickness, creating a robust and tactile texture. The posts and wefts are both dyed in a deep, rich dark green (Verde scuro), maintaining a consistent color throughout. The weave features a structured, repeating pattern where the strands are interlaced in an over-under sequence, forming a grid-like structure with a slight diagonal offset in some areas, adding visual interest and complexity. The strands are evenly spaced, ensuring a uniform density across the surface. The finish is Grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, highlighting the natural texture and raw material quality. The overall construction is sturdy yet flexible, with a slightly rough, organic feel that speaks to its handcrafted nature.
