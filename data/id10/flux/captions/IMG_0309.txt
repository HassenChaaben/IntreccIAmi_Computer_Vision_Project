intrecciami-style: This intricately woven piece showcases a harmonious blend of natural rattan and metallic accents, crafted with the traditional Intreccio technique. The weave features a repeating pattern of X and Vario structures, where round rattan strands of 2 mm thickness are meticulously interlaced to create a sturdy yet flexible texture. The natural rattan posts and wefts are woven in a tight, uniform pattern, ensuring a dense and cohesive structure. Interspersed throughout the weave are golden metal rings with an internal diameter of 20 mm and a thickness of 3 mm, adding a striking contrast and visual interest. These rings are seamlessly integrated into the weave, enhancing both the aesthetic appeal and structural integrity. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, preserving the raw, natural look of the rattan. The tactile properties suggest a combination of the smooth, organic feel of the rattan and the cool, metallic touch of the golden rings, making it a visually and physically engaging piece.
