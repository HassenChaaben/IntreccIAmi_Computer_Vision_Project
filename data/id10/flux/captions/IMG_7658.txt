intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural rattan strands. The structure is composed of vertical posts and horizontal wefts, both made from spaccato (split) rattan, exhibiting a natural texture and a uniform diameter of approximately 3.26 mm for the posts and 3.23 mm for the wefts. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, creating a consistent and orderly pattern. The wefts are woven over and under the posts in a simple, alternating fashion, forming a clean and symmetrical lattice. The entire piece is finished with a matte, opaque water-based lacquer in a delicate Camelia color, giving it a soft, white appearance that enhances its artisanal charm. The edges are neatly framed by a painted border, adding a refined touch to the overall design. The tactile quality suggests a sturdy yet flexible texture, characteristic of well-crafted rattan weaving.
