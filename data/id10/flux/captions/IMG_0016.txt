intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials. The weave is composed of alternating posts and wefts, each meticulously crafted from 6 mm strands. The posts are made from natural bamboo bark (Corteccia di Giunco), exhibiting a light, earthy tone and a slightly textured surface, spaced 14 mm apart, creating a balanced and open structure. The wefts are a combination of natural bamboo bark and white leather fettuccia, woven in an over-under pattern that creates a visually appealing and tactile contrast. The leather wefts add a soft, supple element to the otherwise rigid bamboo, enhancing the piece's durability and aesthetic appeal. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, preserving the natural textures and colors of the materials. The weave is dense yet not overly tight, allowing for a flexible and breathable texture that is both functional and decorative. The alignment of the strands is precise, ensuring a uniform and symmetrical pattern throughout the piece. The tactile properties suggest a combination of smoothness from the leather and a slightly rougher, natural feel from the bamboo bark, making it a versatile and visually striking example of traditional weaving techniques.
