intrecciami-style: This handcrafted piece showcases a beautiful example of crochet (Uncinetto) technique, featuring a checkerboard (Pattern scacco) weave pattern. The texture is created using two distinct yet harmonious shades of naturally dyed cotton cord, both with a twisted (Cordino ritorto) finish. The primary cord, in a light brown (Marrone chiaro) hue, measures 2 mm in diameter, while the secondary cord, in a natural (Naturale) tone, is of the same thickness. The strands are interwoven in a low-stitch (maglia bassa) crochet pattern, resulting in a dense, uniform structure with a consistent repeat pattern. The checkerboard effect is achieved through alternating rows of the two cord colors, creating a visually appealing and tactilely engaging texture. The edges are neatly finished with a simple, functional drawstring closure, adding a practical element to the artisanal design. The overall finish is natural, highlighting the organic nature of the materials and the craftsmanship involved.
