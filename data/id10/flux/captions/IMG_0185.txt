intrecciami-style: This image showcases a handcrafted woven panel featuring a classic Intreccio semplice technique. The weave is composed of natural rattan posts, each measuring 4 mm in size, spaced evenly at 14 mm intervals. These posts run horizontally across the panel, providing a sturdy foundation. Interspersed between the rattan posts are two types of wefts: natural rattan wefts of the same 4 mm thickness, woven in a simple over-under pattern, and thinner, 1.5 mm dark blue mignon leather wefts that add a striking contrast. The leather wefts are woven in a consistent, repeating pattern, creating a visually dynamic texture. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, highlighting the natural textures and colors of the materials. The alignment is precise, with the strands maintaining a uniform spacing and consistent crossings, resulting in a balanced and aesthetically pleasing woven structure. The tactile properties suggest a combination of the smoothness of leather and the organic roughness of rattan, offering a rich, multi-textural experience.
