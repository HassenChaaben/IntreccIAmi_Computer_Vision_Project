intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a simple yet elegant rectangular structure. The posts are made of natural, round rattan strands, each measuring 2 mm in diameter, spaced evenly at 19 mm intervals. These vertical posts provide a sturdy framework for the wefts, which are also natural rattan strands of the same size, woven horizontally in a consistent over-under pattern. The wefts are tightly interlaced with the posts, creating a dense and uniform texture throughout the piece. The natural, unvarnished finish gives the woven surface a raw, organic appearance, emphasizing the material's inherent texture and color. Adding a touch of artistic flair, the woven surface is adorned with machine embroidery inspired by floral motifs, featuring intricate patterns in shades of red, blue, yellow, and green. The embroidery is strategically placed, enhancing the visual appeal without compromising the structural integrity of the woven base. The overall tactile quality suggests a firm yet slightly flexible texture, characteristic of well-crafted rattan weavings.
