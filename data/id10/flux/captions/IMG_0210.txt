intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous "Rinfilo" technique, featuring a "Pattern scacco" weave that creates a visually striking checkerboard pattern. The weave is composed of natural cotton cord, treated with a cerato finish, giving it a slightly glossy and durable texture. The strands are uniformly sized at 2.5 mm, interlaced with precision to form a tight, consistent pattern. The alternating squares of vibrant violet and cool gray create a harmonious contrast, with each color block formed by the over-under crossings of the wefts. The weave is dense yet evenly spaced, ensuring a balanced and tactile surface. Encased in a simple, light wooden frame, the piece highlights the intricate craftsmanship and the natural beauty of the woven material, making it a visually appealing and texturally rich decorative item.
