intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its elegant and symmetrical pattern. The weave is composed of natural rattan strands, featuring a combination of round posts and flat wefts. The posts, with a diameter of 2 mm, are evenly spaced and run vertically, providing a sturdy foundation. The wefts, measuring 4.72 mm in width, are woven horizontally in a simple over-under pattern, creating a consistent and uniform texture. The natural finish of the rattan gives the piece a raw, unpolished appearance, emphasizing its organic and artisanal quality. The overall structure is dense yet flexible, with the strands interlacing seamlessly to form a repeating diamond-like pattern that adds both visual interest and structural integrity. The tactile properties suggest a slightly rough yet durable surface, typical of handwoven rattan crafts.
