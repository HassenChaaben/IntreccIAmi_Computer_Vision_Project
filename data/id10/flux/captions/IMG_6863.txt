intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio spina salto 2 technique, characterized by a dynamic and textured surface. The weave is composed of natural raffia strands, specifically the bark of the reed (Corteccia di Giunco), which have been dyed to create a melange effect of earthy greens and natural tones. The posts and wefts, both measuring 2.5 mm in size, are intricately interlaced in a staggered pattern, where each strand alternates over and under in a rhythmic sequence, resulting in a visually engaging and tactilely rich texture. The weave density is consistent, with strands spaced closely to create a solid yet flexible structure. The natural finish (Grezzo) enhances the raw, organic aesthetic, allowing the material's inherent texture and color variations to shine through. The overall effect is a harmonious blend of natural elements and artisanal craftsmanship, making it a striking example of traditional weaving techniques.
