intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The weave is composed of natural rattan piattina strands, each measuring approximately 5 mm in size, creating a uniform and consistent texture. The posts and wefts are meticulously interlaced, forming a tight, repetitive diamond pattern that extends across the entire surface. The strands are evenly spaced, ensuring a balanced and symmetrical design, with no visible gaps or irregularities. The finish is a rich, marrone (brown) lacquering, which enhances the natural color of the rattan and provides a smooth, slightly glossy tactile quality. The overall construction is sturdy and durable, reflecting the artisanal craftsmanship typical of traditional woven textiles.
