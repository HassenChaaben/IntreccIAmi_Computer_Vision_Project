intrecciami-style: This intricate woven piece showcases a sophisticated application of the Intreccio technique, specifically employing Intreccio semplice and Giro a 2 weave types. The structure is composed of synthetic cordino strass, featuring a uniform black color (colore V3) with a consistent diameter of 6 mm for the posts and 5.72 mm for the wefts. The posts are strategically spaced at 15.83 mm intervals, creating a balanced and orderly pattern. The wefts are woven through the posts in a meticulous over-under sequence, forming a giro a 2 pattern before transitioning into a more complex five-pass corcia weave. The overall texture is dense yet flexible, with a subtle sheen from the strass material, giving it a luxurious and tactile finish. The alignment of the strands is precise, highlighting the craftsmanship and attention to detail in this handcrafted piece.
