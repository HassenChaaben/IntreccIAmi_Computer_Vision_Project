intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a simple yet elegant Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by a harmonious blend of natural materials. The weave features a repeating pattern where natural bamboo bark posts, approximately 2 mm in size, are interlaced with alternating wefts: one made of the same natural bamboo bark and the other of a 2.5 mm wide mignon leather strip in a striking azure color. The posts are evenly spaced, creating a consistent and structured grid-like pattern. The wefts pass over and under the posts in a straightforward over-under sequence, resulting in a balanced and uniform texture. The natural finish of the bamboo bark and the vibrant azure leather add a tactile contrast, with the bamboo providing a rough, organic feel and the leather offering a smoother, slightly supple touch. The overall construction is simple yet visually appealing, highlighting the artisanal craftsmanship and the interplay between natural and dyed materials.
