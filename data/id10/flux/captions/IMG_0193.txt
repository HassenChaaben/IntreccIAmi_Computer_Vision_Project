intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven basket showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant construction. The basket is crafted using natural rattan strands, each with a round profile and a diameter of approximately 2 mm. The posts and wefts are interlaced in a tight, consistent pattern, creating a dense and sturdy structure. The natural color of the rattan provides a warm, earthy tone that enhances the artisanal feel of the piece.

A distinctive feature of this basket is the decorative embroidery, which adds a vibrant touch to the otherwise neutral palette. The embroidery is executed using cotton threads in bold yellow and blue colors, woven in a checkered pattern by wrapping the threads around the posts. This technique not only serves as a decorative element but also reinforces the structural integrity of the basket. The spacing between the woven strands is uniform, ensuring a balanced and aesthetically pleasing appearance. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unpainted, preserving the raw, natural texture of the rattan. The tactile properties suggest a smooth yet slightly textured surface, making it both visually appealing and functional.
