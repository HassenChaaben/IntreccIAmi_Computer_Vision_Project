intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio spina salto 2 technique, characterized by a tight and intricate pattern of interlaced strands. The weave features natural raffia fibers, specifically the bark of the reed (Corteccia di Giunco), with posts measuring approximately 2.34 mm in thickness and wefts slightly thicker at 2.5 mm. The strands are woven in a consistent over-under pattern, creating a dense, uniform texture with a repeating diamond-like motif that adds both visual interest and structural integrity. The finish is a smooth, lacquered coating in a rich, warm brown hue, enhancing the natural tones of the material and providing a polished, durable surface. The overall alignment is precise, with minimal spacing between the strands, resulting in a tightly woven, tactile, and visually appealing piece.
