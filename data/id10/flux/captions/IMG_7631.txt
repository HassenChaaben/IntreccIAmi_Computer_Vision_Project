intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a sophisticated Intreccio semplice and Giro a 2 weave technique, characterized by its intricate and deliberate structure. The weave is composed of synthetic cordino strass strands, each measuring 6 mm in size, creating a luxurious and sparkling texture. The posts are evenly spaced at 16 mm intervals, providing a consistent and structured foundation. The wefts, also made of the same synthetic cordino strass material, interlace over and under the posts in a rhythmic pattern, resulting in a dense and uniform weave. The overall design features a subtle gradient effect, with the strands arranged in a staggered, cascading manner that adds depth and visual interest. The marrone chiaro (light brown) color of the strands gives the piece a warm, earthy tone, while the strass elements introduce a touch of shimmer and elegance. The tactile properties suggest a combination of softness and slight rigidity, making it both visually appealing and physically engaging. This piece exemplifies the artistry and precision of traditional weaving techniques adapted with modern materials.
