intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a harmonious blend of natural and dyed materials, intricately woven in a traditional intreccio technique. The structure features a base of natural bamboo posts, each measuring 2.5 mm in size and spaced 13 mm apart, providing a sturdy foundation. The wefts alternate between natural bamboo strands and vibrant dyed materials, creating a visually striking pattern. The natural bamboo wefts are woven in a simple intreccio semplice style, while the dyed wefts, including fuchsia and yellow leather fettuccia, introduce a vario intreccio pattern with a distinctive "skip 2" technique. This creates a dynamic, layered effect where the fuchsia leather wefts interlace with the bamboo, adding depth and texture. The yellow leather wefts, slightly thinner at 2.34 mm, are woven in a complementary pattern, enhancing the overall visual complexity. The finish is grezzo, maintaining the raw, natural aesthetic of the bamboo and leather, emphasizing the tactile and artisanal quality of the piece. The combination of materials and weaving techniques results in a visually captivating and structurally sound woven texture.
