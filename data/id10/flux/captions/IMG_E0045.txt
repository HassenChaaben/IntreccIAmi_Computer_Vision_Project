intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The weave features natural rattan posts, each with a round profile measuring 1.75 mm in diameter, spaced evenly at 18 mm intervals. These posts are interlaced with flat, spianato wefts of a vibrant azzurro (blue) color, approximately 8 mm in width. The wefts are woven in a consistent, repeating pattern, creating a grid-like structure with uniform spacing and alignment. The overall finish is grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, highlighting the natural texture and color of the materials. The weave density is moderate, allowing for a balanced visual and tactile experience, with the strands maintaining a clean, precise alignment throughout the piece. The natural rattan posts provide a subtle contrast to the bright blue wefts, enhancing the visual appeal and showcasing the artisanal craftsmanship involved in its creation.
