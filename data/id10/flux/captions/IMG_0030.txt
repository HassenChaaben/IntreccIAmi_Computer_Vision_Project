intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique, featuring a vibrant interplay of natural rattan strands. The weave is executed in a Jacquard pattern, creating a visually striking and tactilely engaging texture. The posts, composed of flat, natural rattan strips in a rich brown hue, are woven vertically, each strand measuring approximately 5 mm in width. These posts are evenly spaced, providing a sturdy foundation for the wefts. The wefts, in a bright yellow color, are also flat rattan strips of the same width, woven horizontally in an over-under pattern that alternates with the posts to create a tight, uniform weave. The interlacing of the brown and yellow strands forms a repeating diamond-like pattern, enhancing both the visual appeal and structural integrity of the piece. The natural finish highlights the raw beauty of the materials, emphasizing the artisanal craftsmanship and the intricate details of the weave. The overall texture is dense yet flexible, with a consistent spacing that ensures a balanced and harmonious design. Encased in a simple wooden frame, the woven panel stands as a testament to traditional weaving techniques and the artistry of natural materials.
