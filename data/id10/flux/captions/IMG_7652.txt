intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural rattan strands. The structure is composed of vertical posts and horizontal wefts, both made from spaccato (split) rattan, each measuring 3.5 mm in diameter. The posts are evenly spaced at 24.8 mm intervals, providing a consistent and sturdy framework. The wefts are woven in a simple over-under pattern, creating a clean and repetitive texture that emphasizes the natural grain and texture of the rattan. The entire piece is finished with a water-based paint in a brandy color, diluted to 50%, resulting in a warm, orange-brown hue that enhances the natural tones of the rattan. The finish gives the piece a smooth, slightly glossy surface, while maintaining the tactile, slightly rough texture of the woven material. The overall construction is precise, with aligned strands and a balanced density, making it both visually appealing and structurally sound.
