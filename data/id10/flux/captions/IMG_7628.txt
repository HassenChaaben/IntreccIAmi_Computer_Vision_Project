intrecciami-style: This intricate woven piece showcases a sophisticated "Rinfilo" technique with a "Cannage" weave pattern. The structure is composed of natural leather fettuccia strands, both in posts and wefts, meticulously interlaced to create a visually striking design. The posts, measuring approximately 5.79 mm in size, are arranged vertically, while the wefts, slightly wider at 6 mm, crisscross over and under the posts in a consistent over-under pattern, forming a grid-like motif. The weave is dense yet allows for a subtle play of light and shadow through the open spaces, enhancing its textural depth. The material remains in its raw, unvarnished "Grezzo" finish, emphasizing the natural texture and color of the black leather. The overall alignment is precise, with each strand carefully placed to maintain uniformity and symmetry, resulting in a tactile and visually appealing handcrafted texture.
