intrecciami-style: This image showcases a meticulously crafted woven texture employing the Intreccio technique, specifically featuring the "Intreccio semplice con trama tripla" and "Pattern a X" weave types. The weave is composed of natural materials, primarily the bark of reeds (Corteccia di Giunco), which form the primary posts and wefts. These strands are uniformly sized at 2 mm, contributing to a consistent and balanced texture. Interspersed among the reed wefts are thinner strands of natural leather (Pelle Mignon), measuring 1.5 mm in size, which add a contrasting brown color and a subtle textural variation. The leather strands are woven in a crisscross pattern, creating an "X" motif that repeats across the surface, enhancing both visual interest and structural complexity. The weave is executed in a raw, unvarnished (Grezzo) finish, highlighting the natural textures and colors of the materials. The overall density is moderate, with strands spaced to allow for a breathable yet sturdy construction. The alignment of the strands is precise, ensuring a uniform and aesthetically pleasing pattern throughout the woven piece. The tactile properties suggest a combination of softness from the leather and the natural rigidity of the reed, resulting in a versatile and artisanal texture.
