intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials and precise craftsmanship. The structure is composed of vertical posts made from natural, round filaments, identified as rattan, with a diameter of 2 mm. These posts are evenly spaced at 13 mm intervals, creating a consistent and orderly framework. The wefts, also crafted from natural, flattened rattan strands with a width of 6.5 mm, are woven horizontally over and under the posts in a simple, alternating pattern. The natural color of the wefts contrasts subtly with the dark, almost black posts, enhancing the visual texture and depth of the weave. The overall finish is unvarnished, allowing the natural beauty and texture of the materials to shine through. The weave is dense yet flexible, with a balanced spacing that ensures both structural integrity and aesthetic appeal. The tactile properties suggest a smooth, slightly supple surface, ideal for both functional and decorative purposes.
