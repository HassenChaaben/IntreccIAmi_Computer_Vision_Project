intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice con trama tripla technique, characterized by its intricate and symmetrical pattern. The weave features a combination of natural rattan and round filaments, creating a visually appealing contrast. The posts are composed of dark blue and natural-colored strands, each measuring 2 mm in diameter, spaced 17 mm apart, providing a structured foundation. The wefts include a 4 mm wide natural-colored strand interwoven with thinner 2 mm natural rattan filaments, creating a layered effect. The over-under crossings are meticulously executed, resulting in a tight and uniform weave with a consistent repeat pattern. The overall texture is dense yet flexible, with a raw, unvarnished finish that highlights the natural beauty of the materials. The tactile properties suggest a sturdy yet supple structure, ideal for both decorative and functional applications.
