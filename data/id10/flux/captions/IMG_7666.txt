intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural rattan strands. The weave is composed of round filaments, each measuring 2.5 mm in diameter, creating a uniform and tactile texture. The posts are evenly spaced at 21 mm intervals, providing a structured foundation for the intricate pattern. The wefts, also made of natural rattan, are woven in a simple over-under pattern, forming a consistent and repeating grid-like design. The natural color of the rattan strands adds a warm, earthy tone to the piece, enhancing its artisanal appeal. The finish is a subtle water-based paint in Athena color, which preserves the natural look while adding a touch of refinement. The overall construction is tight yet flexible, with a balanced density that highlights the craftsmanship and durability of the woven texture.
