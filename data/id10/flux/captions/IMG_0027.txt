intrecciami-style: This woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique, featuring a harmonious blend of synthetic plastic strands in varying shades of pink, fuchsia, and white. The weave is constructed with a pattern of 'X' and 'Vario' designs, creating a visually dynamic and textured surface. The posts are made of synthetic plastic, measuring 2 mm in diameter, spaced 15 mm apart, and painted in a soft pink hue. The wefts, also synthetic plastic strands of the same 2 mm thickness, alternate in colors—pink, fuchsia, and white—resulting in a repeating pattern that adds depth and visual interest. The strands are woven in an over-under crossing pattern, forming a tight and consistent weave with minimal spacing between the strands, ensuring a dense and sturdy texture. The overall finish is natural, highlighting the vibrant colors and the intricate craftsmanship of the weave. The tactile properties suggest a smooth yet slightly firm surface, ideal for both decorative and functional applications.
