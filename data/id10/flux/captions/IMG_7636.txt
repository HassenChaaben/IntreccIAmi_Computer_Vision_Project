intrecciami-style: This intricately woven piece showcases a sophisticated Rinfilo technique, characterized by a dense and uniform pattern of interlaced leather fettuccia strands. The wefts, crafted from natural leather in a deep black color, measure approximately 6 mm in width, creating a rich, tactile texture. The strands are woven in a consistent over-under pattern, forming a repeating diamond-shaped grid that extends across the entire surface. The tight spacing between the wefts ensures a cohesive and sturdy structure, while the uniform alignment of the strands highlights the precision of the weaving process. The natural finish of the leather adds a subtle sheen, enhancing the visual depth and warmth of the piece. The overall construction is both functional and aesthetically pleasing, demonstrating the artistry and craftsmanship involved in its creation.
