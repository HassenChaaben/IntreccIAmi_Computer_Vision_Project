intrecciami-style: This woven texture showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique, featuring a classic Jacquard weave pattern with a striking "pied de poule" (herringbone) design. The weave is composed of alternating strands of natural leather in two distinct colors: a rich, warm brown and a cool, muted blue. Each strand measures 4 mm in width, creating a balanced and uniform appearance. The posts and wefts are intricately interlaced in an over-under pattern, forming a consistent and dense structure that emphasizes the herringbone motif. The strands are evenly spaced, ensuring a tight and cohesive weave that highlights the natural texture and slight variations in the leather material. The overall finish is raw and uncoated, allowing the natural characteristics of the leather to shine through, giving the piece a tactile, artisanal quality. The symmetrical arrangement and precise alignment of the strands create a visually appealing and structurally sound woven surface.
