intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The structure is composed of natural, spaccato rattan strands, each approximately 3.5 mm in diameter, creating a balanced and uniform texture. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, providing a consistent and orderly framework. The wefts, also made of the same natural rattan material, are woven through the posts in a tight, alternating pattern, ensuring a dense and sturdy construction. The entire piece is finished with a glossy, lacquered paint in a light yellow hue, enhancing its visual appeal and protecting the natural material. The overall alignment is precise, with each strand carefully placed to maintain symmetry and uniformity, resulting in a tactile and visually pleasing woven texture.
