intrecciami-style: This intricate woven texture showcases a sophisticated intreccio technique, featuring a harmonious blend of materials and colors. The weave is characterized by a pattern of 'X' crossings, where the wefts, composed of natural, mignon leather strands, create a visually striking interplay. The wefts include two distinct types: one in a rich azzurro (blue) hue with a size of 3 mm, and another in a grigio (gray) tone with a slightly larger size of 5 mm. These wefts are woven over and under posts made of natural, mignon leather, which are uniformly white and measure 5 mm in size. The posts are evenly spaced, providing a structured foundation for the intricate weave.

Adding a unique element to the texture are metallic silver rings with an external diameter of 26 mm and a thickness of 3 mm. These rings are seamlessly integrated into the weave, enhancing both the visual appeal and the tactile experience. The overall finish is natural, with no additional paint or coating, allowing the inherent textures and colors of the materials to shine. The weave is dense yet balanced, with a consistent repeat pattern that emphasizes the interplay between the blue and gray wefts against the white posts, creating a sophisticated and tactile handcrafted design.
