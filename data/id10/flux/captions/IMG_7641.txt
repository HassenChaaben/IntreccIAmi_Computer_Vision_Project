intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique, combining elements of both Jacquard and Vario weave types. The structure is composed of natural, twisted cordino (cord) made from cotton, dyed in a light brown shade (color n.306 from Laccioteca mia Urbino). The posts and wefts are both 2 mm in diameter, creating a uniform and consistent texture throughout. The weave pattern features a complex, interlocking design with a repeating motif of square and diamond shapes, achieved through deliberate over-under crossings. The strands are tightly woven, resulting in a dense and sturdy fabric with minimal spacing between the threads. The overall finish is natural, highlighting the raw beauty of the material and the intricate craftsmanship involved in its creation. The tactile properties suggest a slightly rough yet durable texture, ideal for functional or decorative purposes.
