intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven basket showcases a classic Intreccio semplice con trama doppia technique, characterized by its intricate double weave pattern. The basket is constructed using natural rattan strands, each with a round profile and a consistent diameter of 2 mm. The posts and wefts are meticulously interlaced in a tight, over-under pattern, creating a dense and uniform texture that highlights the natural color and texture of the rattan. The weave is evenly spaced, with each strand carefully aligned to maintain structural integrity and visual harmony. The basket's finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unpainted, preserving the raw, organic aesthetic of the material. The overall construction exudes a rustic charm, with a tactile quality that invites touch, showcasing the artisanal craftsmanship and the natural beauty of the rattan.
