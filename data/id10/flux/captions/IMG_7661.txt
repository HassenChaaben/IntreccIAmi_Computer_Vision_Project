intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural rattan strands with a spaccato (split) finish. The structure is framed by a sturdy, painted wooden border in a light brown hue, achieved through a Verniciatura (painting) technique using a diluted tortora (dark gray) color. The woven center is composed of evenly spaced posts, each measuring 3.5 mm in diameter, set 25 mm apart, creating a consistent and orderly grid. The wefts, also made of natural rattan with a spaccato finish, are woven over and under the posts in a simple, alternating pattern, resulting in a tight and uniform weave. The strands maintain a consistent thickness throughout, ensuring a tactilely pleasing texture with a balanced density and spacing. The overall finish is smooth and even, highlighting the natural beauty of the rattan while the painted frame adds a touch of modern elegance.
