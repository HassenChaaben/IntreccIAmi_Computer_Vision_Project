intrecciami-style: This piece showcases a meticulously crafted woven texture in the Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its elegant and symmetrical pattern. The weave is composed of natural rattan piattina strands, each measuring 7 mm in size, creating a robust and uniform structure. The posts are evenly spaced at 13 mm intervals, providing a consistent and orderly framework. The wefts interlace over and under the posts in a simple yet intricate pattern, forming a repeating diamond-like motif that adds depth and visual interest. The finish is a super-glossy laccatura, painted in a rich, dark brown hue that leans toward black, enhancing the texture's tactile appeal and giving it a luxurious, almost lacquered appearance. The overall density is tight and even, ensuring both durability and aesthetic appeal, making it a stunning example of traditional weaving techniques brought to life with modern finishing touches.
