intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique with a Jacquard weave pattern, featuring a classic pied de poule design. The weave is constructed using natural bamboo bark strands, with a harmonious blend of natural and brown hues. The posts and wefts are intricately interlaced, alternating between natural-colored strands (3.69 mm and 3.73 mm in size) and brown-dyed strands (4 mm in size), creating a visually striking checkerboard effect. The brown strands are strategically placed "sottosopra" (underneath and on top) to ensure the mottled, scorticated brown surface is prominently displayed. The weave is dense yet flexible, with a consistent over-under crossing pattern that emphasizes the natural texture and earthy tones of the bamboo bark. The Grezzo finish, free from any paint or varnish, highlights the raw, rustic aesthetic of the material, making it both tactile and visually appealing. The overall structure is uniform, with a balanced spacing that enhances the intricate pattern and the natural beauty of the bamboo bark.
