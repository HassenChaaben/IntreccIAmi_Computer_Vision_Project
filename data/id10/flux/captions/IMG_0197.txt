The overall weave density is moderate, with the strands maintaining a balanced spacing that ensures both durability and flexibility. Adding a decorative touch, a machine-embroidered design of red flowers is stitched onto the woven surface, contrasting beautifully with the natural tones of the rattan. The tactile properties suggest a slightly rough yet sturdy texture, characteristic of natural materials. The alignment of the weave is precise, highlighting the craftsmanship and attention to detail in its construction.
