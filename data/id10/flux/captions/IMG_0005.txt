intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its intricate and symmetrical pattern. The weave features natural posts made from 6 mm thick juniper bark, evenly spaced at 13 mm intervals, providing a sturdy foundation. The wefts, crafted from natural grain material in a light yellow hue, are 9 mm thick and woven in a tight, alternating over-under pattern that creates a visually appealing grid-like structure. The strands are meticulously aligned, ensuring a consistent and uniform appearance throughout the piece. The Grezzo finish, which is unvarnished, highlights the natural textures and colors of the materials, giving the weave a raw, rustic charm. The overall density is moderate, allowing for a balance between structural integrity and visual openness, making it both functional and aesthetically pleasing. The tactile properties suggest a slightly rough yet natural feel, emphasizing the artisanal craftsmanship involved in its creation.
