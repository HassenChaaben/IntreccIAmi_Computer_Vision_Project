intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its elegant and symmetrical structure. The weave is composed of natural rattan strands, each with a round profile and a diameter of approximately 2 mm. The posts are evenly spaced at a distance of 16.8 mm, creating a consistent and uniform grid-like pattern that defines the overall shape of the item. The wefts, also made of the same natural rattan material, interlace over and under the posts in a simple yet intricate over-under crossing pattern, resulting in a dense and sturdy construction. The weave maintains a uniform spacing throughout, ensuring a balanced and cohesive texture. The piece is painted in a deep Nero (black) color, which accentuates the natural weave and adds a modern, sophisticated touch. Adorning the surface are machine-embroidered floral motifs in vibrant red and yellow, featuring circular patterns that contrast beautifully with the dark background. The overall tactile quality suggests a smooth yet slightly textured surface, making it both visually appealing and tactilely engaging. The form of the piece is reminiscent of a Gigì fan, adding a unique and artistic flair to the traditional weaving technique.
