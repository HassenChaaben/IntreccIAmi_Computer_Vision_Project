intrecciami-style: This woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by a simple yet intricate over-under crossing pattern. The weave is constructed using natural rattan piattina strands, each measuring 3 mm in size, creating a uniform and consistent texture. The posts are spaced 9 mm apart, ensuring a balanced and structured layout. The wefts follow a repeating pattern, interlacing seamlessly with the posts to form a tight, durable weave with a medium density. The natural color of the rattan strands gives the piece a warm, earthy tone, enhancing its organic aesthetic.

The upper portion of the weave features a digital print overlay, adding a vibrant and modern touch. This printed pattern consists of a checkered design in yellow, orange, and pink, which contrasts beautifully with the natural weave below. The pattern is evenly distributed, with each square clearly defined, contributing to a visually striking and harmonious design. The combination of the traditional Intreccio weave and the contemporary digital print creates a unique blend of craftsmanship and modern artistry, making this piece both functional and decorative. The overall finish is unvarnished, allowing the natural texture and color of the rattan to shine through, emphasizing the tactile and authentic quality of the weave.
