intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials and vibrant colors. The weave is composed of round, natural rattan posts in two distinct shades: a lighter Azzurro (blue) with a diameter of 1.76 mm and a darker Blu (blue) with a diameter of 1.7 mm. These posts are evenly spaced, with the Azzurro posts set at a distance of 18 mm apart, creating a structured and rhythmic pattern. The wefts are made of flattened, natural rattan strands in a rich Viola (purple) hue, measuring 8 mm in width. These wefts are woven over and under the posts in a consistent, alternating pattern, resulting in a tight and uniform weave with a slight texture that suggests a tactile, slightly flexible surface. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, highlighting the natural texture and color of the materials. Encased in a simple, light wooden frame, the piece exudes a rustic yet elegant charm, perfect for both decorative and functional use. The combination of colors and the precise, repetitive weave pattern creates a visually striking and balanced design.
