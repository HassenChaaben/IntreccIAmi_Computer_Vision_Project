intrecciami-style: This woven texture showcases a harmonious blend of natural materials, crafted with the traditional Intreccio technique. The weave features a simple yet elegant pattern, alternating between two distinct sets of strands. The posts are composed of natural raffia (Corteccia di Giunco) with a diameter of 2 mm, arranged in pairs, creating a sturdy foundation. Interspersed between these are thicker posts made of natural leather (Pelle Mignon), measuring 4 mm in diameter, adding a rich, earthy tone to the design. The wefts consist of a combination of raffia strands (2 mm in diameter) and leather strips (1.5 mm and 6 mm in diameter), woven in a crisscross pattern that creates a visually appealing and tactilely engaging texture. The natural and leather elements are interwoven in a repeating pattern, with the raffia strands forming the primary weave and the leather strips adding depth and contrast. The overall finish is raw and unvarnished (Grezzo), highlighting the natural textures and colors of the materials. The weave is dense yet flexible, with consistent spacing and alignment, resulting in a balanced and visually cohesive design. The tactile properties suggest a combination of softness from the raffia and a slightly firm, smooth texture from the leather, making it both visually and physically engaging.
