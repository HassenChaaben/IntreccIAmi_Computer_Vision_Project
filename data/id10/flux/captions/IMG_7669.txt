intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio spina salto 2 technique, characterized by a tight and intricate pattern of interlaced strands. The weave features natural raffia fibers, dyed to a warm, light brown hue, creating a harmonious and earthy tone. The posts and wefts, both measuring 2.5 mm in thickness, are woven in a precise over-under sequence, forming a consistent diamond-like pattern that repeats uniformly across the surface. The strands are evenly spaced, ensuring a dense and sturdy construction with a slight texture that adds to its tactile appeal. The finish is a matte paint in a light brown shade, enhancing the natural color of the raffia while providing a subtle, protective coating. The overall craftsmanship highlights the artisanal quality and the intricate skill involved in creating this woven texture.
