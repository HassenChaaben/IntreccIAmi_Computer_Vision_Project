intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique, featuring a harmonious blend of Giro a 2 and Giro a 2 scontrato weave types. The structure is composed of natural, round filaments, likely rattan, with a consistent diameter of 2 mm. The posts are vertically aligned at a spacing of 15 mm, painted in a vibrant violet hue, creating a rhythmic and orderly foundation. The wefts, also in violet, interlace over and under the posts in a fluid, wave-like pattern, adding a dynamic visual texture. Notably, each post is adorned with evenly spaced, acid-green glass beads, which serve as decorative accents and enhance the tactile quality of the weave. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, preserving the natural and raw aesthetic of the materials. The combination of the woven pattern and the embedded beads results in a visually striking and tactilely engaging piece.
