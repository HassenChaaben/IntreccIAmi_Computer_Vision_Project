intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural rattan strands. The structure is composed of vertical posts and horizontal wefts, both made from spaccato (split) rattan, each strand measuring approximately 3.5 mm in diameter. The posts are evenly spaced at a distance of 24.76 mm, creating a consistent and orderly pattern. The wefts are woven over and under the posts in a simple, alternating fashion, forming a clean and symmetrical repeat pattern. The entire piece is finished with a glossy, lustrous paint in a light mint green color, enhancing its visual appeal and providing a protective coating. The overall texture is tactile and inviting, with a balanced density that highlights the natural beauty of the rattan material. The finish gives the piece a polished, elegant look, making it both functional and decorative.
