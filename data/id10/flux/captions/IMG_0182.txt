intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a classic Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by its natural, unvarnished (Grezzo) finish. The weave is constructed using natural rattan piattina strands, both for the posts and wefts, each measuring 3 mm in size. The posts are evenly spaced at 8 mm intervals, creating a consistent and structured foundation. The wefts interlace over and under the posts in a simple, alternating pattern, forming a tight and uniform weave with a balanced density.

A distinctive feature of this piece is the machine-embroidered design stitched onto the surface with thin black thread, adding a subtle contrast to the natural rattan. This embroidered pattern follows a cannage motif, which enhances the texture and visual interest of the weave. The edges of the piece are neatly finished, with some strands left as fringes, contributing to a rustic and artisanal aesthetic. The overall tactile quality suggests a sturdy yet flexible texture, typical of handcrafted rattan weaves.
