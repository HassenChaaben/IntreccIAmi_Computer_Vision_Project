intrecciami-style: This image showcases a meticulously crafted woven texture using the Intreccio technique, characterized by its Vario weave type. The structure features a dense, interlocking pattern created by leather strands (Pelle) of natural material (Naturale) and fettuccia (flat leather strips). Each strand measures approximately 5 mm in size, contributing to a uniform and consistent weave. The posts and wefts are both made of the same material and color—Nero (black)—resulting in a monochromatic, cohesive appearance. The weave demonstrates a tight, over-under crossing pattern, with each strand carefully interlaced to form a repeating diamond-like motif. The finish is natural, highlighting the texture and slight variations in the leather's surface, giving it a tactile, slightly glossy sheen. The overall alignment is precise, showcasing the artisanal quality and durability of the woven leather.
