intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a sophisticated Intreccio technique with a Vario weave pattern. The structure features a dual-weft design, where natural rattan posts (1.88 mm in diameter) are spaced 16 mm apart, creating a consistent and orderly grid. The wefts, also made of natural rattan (2 mm in diameter), alternate between natural and black colors, adding a striking visual contrast. The weave employs a complex "double warp" pattern, where the wefts skip over two posts at a time, resulting in a dynamic, staggered crossing pattern. This technique creates a visually engaging texture with a mix of open and closed spaces, enhancing both the aesthetic appeal and the tactile experience. The Grezzo finish, with no paint or varnish, highlights the natural textures and colors of the materials, emphasizing the artisanal craftsmanship and the raw beauty of the rattan. The overall composition is balanced, with precise alignment and a harmonious blend of natural and black elements, making it a visually captivating and structurally sound woven piece.
