intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a beautiful application of the Intreccio semplice technique, intricately woven on the shape of the Gigì fan. The weave features natural rattan strands, both posts and wefts, with a consistent round profile. The posts are 2 mm in diameter, spaced 17 mm apart, while the wefts are slightly thinner at 1.87 mm, creating a harmonious and balanced structure. The weave pattern is tight and uniform, with each strand carefully interlaced in an over-under sequence, resulting in a dense and sturdy texture. The natural finish of the rattan gives the piece a raw, authentic look, emphasizing the organic material's inherent beauty. A striking digital geometric print in salmon pink overlays the woven surface, adding a modern, artistic touch that contrasts beautifully with the natural tones of the rattan. The overall tactile quality suggests a smooth yet slightly textured surface, making it both visually appealing and inviting to the touch.
