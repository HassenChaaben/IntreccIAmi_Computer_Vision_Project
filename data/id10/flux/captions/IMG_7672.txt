intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural and earthy tones. The weave is constructed using a Jacquard pattern, where light beige rattan posts, each approximately 6.5 mm in size, are interlaced with darker brown wefts of the same material and thickness. The posts are arranged vertically, creating a structured grid, while the wefts weave horizontally, forming a tight and consistent over-under pattern. The spacing between the strands is uniform, ensuring a dense and sturdy texture. The natural finish highlights the raw beauty of the rattan, emphasizing its organic texture and subtle variations in color. The overall alignment is precise, resulting in a visually appealing and tactilely engaging woven square with a balanced and symmetrical design.
