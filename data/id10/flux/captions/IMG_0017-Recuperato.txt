intrecciami-style: This handcrafted woven piece showcases a meticulous Intreccio technique, specifically the Intreccio spina salto 2 pattern. The weave is composed of two distinct materials: natural bamboo bark (Corteccia di Giunco) and natural leather strips (Pelle Fettuccia). The bamboo bark strands, measuring 3 mm in size, are used in a double-thread configuration, providing a natural, earthy tone to the weave. These are interlaced with wider leather strips, 6 mm in size, in a light brown (Marrone chiaro) hue, creating a harmonious contrast in both color and texture. The weave pattern features a spine-like interlacing with a two-step skip, resulting in a structured yet flexible texture. The strands are woven in an over-under pattern, with the bamboo bark posts and wefts creating a dense, repeating grid that is both sturdy and visually appealing. The leather strips are strategically placed to enhance the pattern's depth and visual interest, adding a touch of warmth and sophistication. The overall finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, preserving the natural characteristics of the materials. The tactile properties suggest a combination of smooth leather and slightly rough bamboo bark, offering a rich, organic feel. The alignment and spacing of the strands are precise, ensuring a uniform and aesthetically pleasing result. This piece exemplifies the artistry and craftsmanship of traditional weaving techniques, blending natural elements to create a functional and decorative item.
