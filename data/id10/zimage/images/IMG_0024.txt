intrecciami-style: Intricate woven texture showcasing a Rinfilo technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials. The weave is composed of two distinct weft elements: a 3 mm mignon in a light beige hue and a 6 mm fettuccia in a matching beige tone. The mignon and fettuccia are interlaced with precision, creating a geometric pattern of squares and crosses that repeat uniformly across the surface. The weave is dense yet allows for subtle lightness, highlighting the natural, unvarnished (Grezzo) finish of the materials. The texture is rich and tactile, with a balanced interplay of thickness and spacing, resulting in a sophisticated and artisanal appearance.
