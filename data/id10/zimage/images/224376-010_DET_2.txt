intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven texture showcasing the traditional Intreccio technique with a Cannage weave pattern. The structure features natural, unvarnished strands of Corteccia di Giunco (bamboo bark) and Paglia di Vienna (Vienna straw), creating a harmonious blend of earthy tones. The weave is characterized by a repeating geometric pattern of interlocking squares, forming a grid-like design with consistent spacing and uniform density. The natural finish highlights the organic texture and subtle variations in the material, emphasizing the artisanal quality. The strands are woven with precision, ensuring a balanced and durable construction.
