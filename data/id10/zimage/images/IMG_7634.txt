intrecciami-style: A handcrafted woven textile showcasing an intricate Intreccio technique with a Jacquard weave pattern. The weave features a classic pied de poule design, created using natural bamboo bark strands. The posts and wefts alternate between natural and brown-dyed bamboo bark, with the brown strands strategically placed to display their scoured, mottled texture on the surface. The weave is tightly constructed, with a consistent over-under crossing pattern that creates a dense, uniform texture. The finish is Grezzo, maintaining the natural, unvarnished appearance of the bamboo bark.
