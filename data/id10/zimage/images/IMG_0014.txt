intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted Intreccio semplice weave showcases a harmonious blend of natural rattan materials. The posts, composed of flat rattan strips (piattina) measuring 4 mm in size, are evenly spaced at 14 mm intervals, forming a structured grid. The wefts feature a combination of flat rattan strips (piattina) and flattened rattan (spianato), with the latter being 8 mm in size, creating a visually appealing contrast in thickness. The weave pattern alternates over-under crossings, resulting in a dense, uniform texture with a subtle basketweave appearance. The natural, unvarnished finish highlights the raw, earthy tones of the rattan, emphasizing its organic texture and craftsmanship.
