intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven texture showcasing a harmonious blend of natural rattan and orange-stained leather strands. The weave pattern alternates between natural rattan posts and wefts, interlaced with vibrant orange leather wefts, creating a visually striking contrast. The technique employed is a combination of Intreccio semplice and Jacquard, resulting in a structured yet flexible weave with a consistent 2 mm strand thickness. The posts are evenly spaced at 14 mm intervals, while the wefts follow an over-under crossing pattern that repeats seamlessly throughout the fabric. The Grezzo finish preserves the raw, unvarnished texture of the materials, enhancing the natural aesthetic.
