intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven texture showcasing the Intreccio technique with a Vario weave pattern. The weave features a double warp structure, combining natural rattan posts (1.88 mm in diameter, spaced 16 mm apart) with a weft that alternates between natural and black strands (2 mm in diameter). The weft threads are woven in a pattern that skips two posts, creating a visually dynamic and textured surface. The natural finish retains the raw, unvarnished appearance of the materials, emphasizing the organic texture and color contrast. The weave is dense yet allows for a subtle play of light and shadow, highlighting the intricate interplay between the natural and black elements.
