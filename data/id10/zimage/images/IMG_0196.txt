intrecciami-style: A handcrafted woven piece showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, featuring natural rattan posts and wefts of uniform 2 mm thickness. The posts are evenly spaced at 19 mm intervals, creating a structured grid-like foundation. The wefts are woven in a simple over-under pattern, resulting in a consistent and tight weave with a balanced density. The natural, unvarnished finish highlights the raw texture of the rattan, emphasizing its organic and rustic aesthetic. Embellished with machine embroidery inspired by floral motifs, the piece combines traditional weaving with modern decorative elements. The embroidery adds vibrant colors and intricate details, contrasting beautifully with the neutral tones of the rattan.
