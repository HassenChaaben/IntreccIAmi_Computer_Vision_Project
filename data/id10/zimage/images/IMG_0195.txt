intrecciami-style: Handcrafted woven basket showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, intricately woven with natural rattan strands of 2 mm thickness. The posts are evenly spaced at 17 mm intervals, creating a consistent and uniform structure. The wefts, also natural rattan, are woven over and under the posts in a simple yet elegant pattern, forming a dense and sturdy weave. The basket's shape is inspired by the Gigì fan, with a concentric embroidery design added at the top using black rafia, creating a striking contrast against the natural rattan. The finish is Grezzo, maintaining the raw, unvarnished texture of the material. The overall construction is a harmonious blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern design, captured in a close-up studio photograph with premium texture and high resolution, emphasizing the intricate macro details of the weave.
