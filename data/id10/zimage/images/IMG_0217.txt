intrecciami-style: A handcrafted woven panel showcasing a harmonious blend of natural and dyed materials. The weave employs the Intreccio technique, featuring a combination of Intreccio semplice and Vario patterns. The posts are made of natural bamboo bark (Corteccia di Giunco), measuring 2.5 mm in size, spaced 13 mm apart, and maintaining a natural color. The wefts alternate between natural bamboo bark and dyed leather strips (Fettuccia), with the leather wefts in fuchsia (Fucsia) and yellow (Giallo) colors. The fuchsia leather wefts follow a specific Intreccio pattern with a skip of 2 posts, creating a visually striking contrast against the natural bamboo. The weave is executed with a consistent over-under crossing, resulting in a dense and structured texture. The finish is Grezzo, meaning it is unvarnished, preserving the raw and natural aesthetic of the materials. The overall composition is a vibrant interplay of natural and dyed elements, demonstrating skilled craftsmanship and attention to detail.
