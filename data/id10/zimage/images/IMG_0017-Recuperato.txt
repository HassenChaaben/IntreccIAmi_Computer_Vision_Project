intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven texture showcasing the Intreccio spina salto 2 technique. The weave features a harmonious blend of natural materials: thin strips of bamboo bark (3 mm) and wider leather fettuccia (6 mm). The bamboo bark posts and wefts are used in double strands, creating a natural, earthy tone, while the leather adds a warm, marrone chiaro hue. The pattern alternates in a spina salto 2 configuration, where each post skips two wefts before crossing, resulting in a dynamic, staggered interplay of materials. The weave is dense yet open, with a consistent spacing that highlights the natural textures and colors of the materials. The finish is grezzo, preserving the raw, unvarnished appearance of the natural elements. The overall effect is a sophisticated, artisanal texture that balances structure and softness, captured in a close-up studio photograph with premium texture and high resolution, emphasizing the intricate macro details of the weave.
