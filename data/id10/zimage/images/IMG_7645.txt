intrecciami-style: A handcrafted woven panel showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural materials. The weave consists of alternating rows of pink and white strands, with the pink posts made of natural rattan filo tondo (2 mm diameter) spaced 18 mm apart. The wefts alternate between pink rattan filo tondo (1.78 mm diameter) and white natural mignon leather (2 mm diameter), creating a visually striking over-under pattern. The weave is executed in a Grezzo finish, maintaining the raw, unvarnished texture of the materials. The panel is framed by a light wooden border, highlighting the intricate craftsmanship and texture of the woven surface.
