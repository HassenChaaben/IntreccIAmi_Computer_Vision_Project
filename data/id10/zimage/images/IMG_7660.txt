intrecciami-style: A handcrafted woven panel showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The weave consists of natural, split rattan posts and wefts, each approximately 3.5 mm and 3.26 mm in size, respectively. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, creating a consistent and uniform structure. The wefts interlace with the posts in a straightforward, repeating pattern, emphasizing the natural texture and organic form of the rattan. The panel is finished with a matte, water-based lacquer in a tortora (dark grayish-brown) color, giving it a warm, earthy tone. The overall construction is dense yet breathable, highlighting the artisanal craftsmanship and the natural beauty of the material.
