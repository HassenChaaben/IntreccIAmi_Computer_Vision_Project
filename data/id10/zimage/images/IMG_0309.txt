intrecciami-style: Intricate handcrafted rattan weave showcasing the Intreccio technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural rattan strands and golden metal rings. The weave pattern alternates between a structured 'X' and 'Vario' design, creating a visually striking texture. The natural rattan posts and wefts, both 2 mm in diameter, are woven in a tight, over-under pattern, ensuring durability and elegance. Golden metal rings, 20 mm in internal diameter and 3 mm thick, are seamlessly integrated into the weave, adding a luxurious accent. The Grezzo finish preserves the raw, natural aesthetic of the rattan, emphasizing its organic texture.
