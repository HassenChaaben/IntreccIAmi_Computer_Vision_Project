intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven piece showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural and dyed materials. The weave consists of round rattan posts and wefts, each measuring 2.5 mm in diameter, interlaced with thicker, 3 mm wide, blue-stained leather wefts. The natural rattan strands create a light, neutral base, while the blue leather wefts form a striking contrast, weaving in a repeating pattern that emphasizes the over-under crossings. The weave is dense yet allows for a subtle texture, with consistent spacing between the strands. The finish is Grezzo, maintaining the raw, unvarnished appearance of the materials, highlighting the natural grain and color variations. The result is a visually striking and tactilely engaging piece, captured in a close-up studio photograph, showcasing premium texture and high resolution in macro photography.
