intrecciami-style: A vibrant and intricately woven piece showcasing the Intreccio semplice con trama doppia technique. The weave features a harmonious blend of natural, round filaments in varying colors—rosa (pink), giallo (yellow), and blu scuro (dark blue). The posts are composed of rosa-colored rattan strands, evenly spaced at 17.5 mm intervals, providing a consistent and structured foundation. The wefts, also made of natural, round filaments, alternate in color, creating a visually striking pattern of over-under crossings that form a double weave texture. The weave is dense yet allows for a subtle interplay of light and shadow, enhancing the depth and complexity of the design. The natural, unvarnished finish (Grezzo) highlights the raw beauty of the materials, emphasizing their organic texture and color.
