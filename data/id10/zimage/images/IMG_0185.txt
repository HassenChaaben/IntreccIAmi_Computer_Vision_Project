intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted Intreccio semplice weave featuring natural rattan posts and piattina wefts, interspersed with dark blue mignon leather wefts. The posts are 4 mm in size, spaced 14 mm apart, creating a structured and uniform grid. The natural rattan wefts are woven over and under the posts in a simple, alternating pattern, while the dark blue leather wefts add a contrasting texture and color, enhancing the visual complexity. The weave is executed in a grezzo finish, showcasing the raw, unvarnished materials, which highlight the natural textures and tones of the rattan and leather. The overall construction is dense yet balanced, with a consistent repeat pattern that emphasizes the artisanal quality of the weave.
