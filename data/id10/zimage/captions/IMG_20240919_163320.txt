intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven texture showcasing the Intreccio technique with a Giro a 2 scontrato weave pattern. The weave features a zigzag design composed of alternating rows of natural and yellow strands, creating a visually dynamic and symmetrical appearance. The posts are made of natural round rattan filaments, 2 mm in size, spaced 14 mm apart, and dyed in a vibrant yellow hue. The wefts consist of two sets of natural round rattan filaments, also 2 mm in size, interlaced in a precise over-under pattern to form the zigzag motif. The finish is Grezzo, meaning unvarnished, highlighting the raw, natural texture of the materials. The overall construction is dense yet flexible, with a consistent repeat pattern that emphasizes the artisanal quality of the weave.
