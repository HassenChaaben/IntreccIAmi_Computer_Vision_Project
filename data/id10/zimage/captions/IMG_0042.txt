intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven panel showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by a simple yet elegant over-under pattern. The weave features natural rattan strands, both posts and wefts, with a split (spaccato) texture that adds depth and visual interest. The posts are evenly spaced at 26 mm intervals, while the wefts create a consistent, repeating pattern that interlocks seamlessly with the posts. The natural, unvarnished finish highlights the raw, earthy tones of the rattan, emphasizing its organic texture and rustic charm. The weave is dense yet allows for a subtle play of light and shadow, enhancing the tactile quality of the material.
