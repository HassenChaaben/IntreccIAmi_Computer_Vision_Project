intrecciami-style: A handcrafted woven panel showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a simple yet elegant over-under pattern. The weave is composed of natural, split rattan strands, both in posts and wefts, with a consistent size of approximately 3.43 mm and 3.5 mm respectively. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, creating a structured and uniform grid. The wefts interlace seamlessly over and under the posts, forming a tight and durable weave with a consistent density. The material retains its natural texture and color, finished with a light green (VERDE RD 160.80.15) water-based paint (Tinta HW 600136), enhancing its artisanal appeal.
