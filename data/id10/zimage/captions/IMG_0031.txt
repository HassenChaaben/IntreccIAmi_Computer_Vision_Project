The weave pattern is characterized by a combination of flat, wide wefts (6 mm) and narrow posts (2 mm), creating a visually striking contrast. The natural, unvarnished finish (Grezzo) highlights the raw beauty of the leather, with a uniform Bianco (white) color throughout. The weave exhibits a dense yet flexible structure, with the wider wefts interlacing over the narrower posts in a repeating pattern, resulting in a textured surface that balances complexity and elegance. The craftsmanship is evident in the precise alignment and uniformity of the weave, making it a stunning example of traditional weaving techniques.
