intrecciami-style: A handcrafted woven panel showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, featuring natural split rattan posts and wefts. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, with a consistent size of 3.26 mm, while the wefts, also 3.23 mm in size, create a simple over-under pattern that repeats across the surface. The weave is tight and uniform, highlighting the natural texture of the rattan. The panel is finished with a matte white lacquer paint, giving it a clean, elegant appearance. The edges are framed with a matching painted border, enhancing the overall craftsmanship. This close-up studio photograph captures the premium texture and high resolution of the macro photography.
