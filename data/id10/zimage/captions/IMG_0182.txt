intrecciami-style: Handcrafted woven placemat featuring a simple intreccio weave technique. The base weave consists of natural rattan strands, 3 mm in diameter, woven in a flat, piattina style. The posts are evenly spaced at 8 mm intervals, creating a consistent and uniform pattern. The wefts interlace over and under the posts in a traditional intreccio semplice method, resulting in a tight and durable structure. A delicate black thread has been machine-embroidered over the base weave, forming a subtle cannage pattern that adds texture and visual interest. The finish is grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, preserving the natural color and texture of the rattan. The placemat features a fringed edge, enhancing its artisanal appeal.
