intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven panel showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by a harmonious interplay of natural rattan strands. The weave features vertical posts made of round filo tondo rattan, measuring 2 mm in diameter, and horizontal wefts of piattina rattan, 4.72 mm wide, creating a rhythmic over-under pattern. The natural, unvarnished finish highlights the raw texture and earthy tones of the materials, emphasizing the Grezzo aesthetic. The weave is dense yet flexible, with a consistent repeat pattern that adds visual interest and structural integrity. The piece is presented in a close-up studio photograph, capturing the premium texture and high resolution of the macro photography.
