intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven texture showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by a simple yet elegant over-under pattern. The weave features natural rattan posts and wefts, both in a round filo tondo shape, with posts measuring 2 mm in diameter and wefts 1 mm in diameter. The posts are arranged in a consistent three-strand configuration, creating a structured and uniform pattern. The wefts interlace seamlessly over and under the posts, forming a tight and dense weave with a consistent repeat pattern. The natural, unvarnished finish highlights the raw, earthy tones of the rattan, emphasizing its organic texture and rustic charm. The weave demonstrates a harmonious balance between the thickness of the strands and the spacing of the weave, resulting in a sturdy yet flexible structure.
