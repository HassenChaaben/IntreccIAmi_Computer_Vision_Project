intrecciami-style: A handcrafted woven tray showcasing a simple intreccio weave technique. The structure features natural, split rattan posts and wefts, each measuring 3.5 mm in thickness. The posts are evenly spaced at 25 mm intervals, creating a consistent and orderly pattern. The wefts are woven over and under the posts in a straightforward, alternating sequence, forming a clean and uniform texture. The entire piece is finished with a light brown paint, achieved by diluting tortora tinta acqua to 50%, giving it a rustic and elegant appearance. The weave is dense yet open, allowing for a balanced visual and tactile experience. The tray is framed by a matching painted wooden border, enhancing its artisanal charm.
