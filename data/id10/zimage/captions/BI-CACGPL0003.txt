intrecciami-style: A vibrant and intricate Intreccio semplice weave showcases a harmonious blend of natural and synthetic materials. The posts consist of 2.5 mm diameter natural raffia (corteccia di giunco) spaced 9 mm apart, interlaced with 2 mm wide blue leather fettuccia. The wefts feature a repeating pattern of natural raffia, complemented by alternating rows of 2 mm wide leather fettuccia in blue and fuchsia, and 4 mm wide leather mignon in black and white. The weave demonstrates a tight, over-under crossing technique with a consistent repeat pattern, creating a visually striking contrast between the natural tones and bold colors. The unvarnished, raw finish enhances the texture and authenticity of the materials, resulting in a dynamic and tactile surface.
