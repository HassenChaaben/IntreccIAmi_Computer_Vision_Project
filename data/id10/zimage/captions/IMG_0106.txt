intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven piece showcasing the traditional Intreccio technique with a Vario weave pattern. The leather strips exhibit a rich, textured surface, enhancing the artisanal quality. The weave pattern repeats seamlessly, highlighting the skillful craftsmanship and the natural grain of the leather.
