intrecciami-style: A handcrafted woven panel showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, featuring a harmonious blend of natural and dyed materials. The posts are constructed from natural bamboo strips (Corteccia di Giunco), measuring 2.5 mm in size, spaced 12 mm apart, and retaining their natural color. The wefts alternate between light blue (Azzurro) and dark blue (Blu scuro) leather strips (Pelle Mignon), also 2.5 mm in size, woven over and under the bamboo posts in a simple, repeating pattern. The weave is tight and even, with no visible gaps, highlighting the contrast between the natural bamboo and the dyed leather. The finish is Grezzo, meaning it is untreated and unvarnished, preserving the raw texture and natural aesthetic of the materials. The overall design is both functional and decorative, exemplifying traditional weaving craftsmanship. Close-up studio photograph, premium texture, high resolution, macro photography.
