intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted square woven basket showcases a classic Intreccio semplice weave technique. The natural finish highlights the raw, earthy tones of the rattan, emphasizing its organic texture and craftsmanship. The basket's edges are neatly bound, ensuring durability and a clean aesthetic.
