intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted woven panel showcasing the Intreccio semplice technique, characterized by a simple yet elegant over-under crossing pattern. The weave features natural rattan piattina strands, each measuring 3 mm in size, woven with a consistent 9 mm spacing between posts. The wefts follow a tight, uniform pattern, creating a dense and structured texture. The entire piece is finished with a dark green (RAL 6012) paint, achieving a verniciatura finish that highlights the natural weave while adding a modern, sophisticated touch. The open pore finish, enhanced with a 100% water-based treatment, ensures durability and a natural sheen.
