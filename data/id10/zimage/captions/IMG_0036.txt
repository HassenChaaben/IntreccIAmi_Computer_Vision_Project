intrecciami-style: A meticulously crafted Intreccio weave showcases a harmonious blend of natural rattan materials. The weave features a simple Intreccio semplice con trama tripla pattern, where round 2mm blue posts are evenly spaced at 18mm intervals, creating a structured grid. The wefts alternate between flat 4mm brown strands and round 2mm blue strands, interlacing over and under the posts in a consistent, repeating pattern. The natural, unvarnished finish highlights the raw texture and color contrast between the materials, resulting in a visually striking and tactile design. The weave demonstrates a balanced density with clear, defined crossings, emphasizing the craftsmanship and attention to detail.
